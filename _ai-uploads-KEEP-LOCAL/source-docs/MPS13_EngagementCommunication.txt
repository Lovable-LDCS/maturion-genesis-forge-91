Minimum Performance Standard for Stakeholder Engagement and Effective Communication. Defines the intent, required actions, and guidance for fostering a security-aware culture by engaging all personnel, promoting transparent reporting, building trust, and supporting collaborative risk management. Emphasises clear, two-way communication channels, employee engagement, leadership accountability, and partnerships with external stakeholders to reduce risks and protect organisational assets.
