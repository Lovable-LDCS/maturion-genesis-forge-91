Sorting is the fifth stage in the diamond recovery pipeline, following Processing. Diamonds recovered are examined, weighed, classified, valued, and packaged securely. This stage is highly sensitive to risks of theft, substitution, or collusion. Strong custody and reconciliation procedures are critical.
