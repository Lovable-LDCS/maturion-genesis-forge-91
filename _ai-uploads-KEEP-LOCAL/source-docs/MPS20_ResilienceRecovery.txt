Minimum Performance Standard for Resilience and Recovery. Defines the intent, required actions, and guidance for ensuring the organisation has structured, risk-informed plans and capabilities to identify, mitigate, control, and recover from incidents that disrupt operations. Emphasises integration with enterprise risk management, comprehensive business continuity planning (BCP), stakeholder coordination, and continuous improvement to build resilience.
