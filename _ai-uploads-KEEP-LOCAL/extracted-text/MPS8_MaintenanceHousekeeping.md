Minimum Performance Standard for Maintenance and Housekeeping. Defines the intent, required actions, and guidance to ensure facilities, equipment, and work environments are maintained in safe, secure, and efficient condition through structured maintenance and housekeeping programs. Supports operational integrity, safety, quality, and security by reducing risks of loss, fraud, inefficiency, or unsafe practices. Aligns with international standards and promotes a culture of accountability, including Visible Felt Leadership (VFL) to demonstrate management commitment and reinforce high standards.
